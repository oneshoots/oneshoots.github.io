5. Naging silungan at nagsilbing tahanan ng mga batang ulila noong panahon ng epidemya ng kolera at beri beri noong 1882. Dito rin nalimbag ang pahayagang "La Independencia o La Libertad", ang diyaryo ng rebolusyon o pag-aalsa. a.) Asilo De Huerfanos b.) Kasilo De Merfanos c.) Silo De Werfanos d.) Wilo De Fuerhanos


ASILO DE HUERFANOS

This is  Asilo De Huerfanos, a significant reminder of Malabon's historical history despite having lost much of its former magnificence.

Following a powerful typhoon that hit Malabon on year 1882, which swept down concrete buildings' roofs and caused cholera and beri-beri outbreaks in November of that same year.

The authorities had to excavate a sizable hole to act as a common tomb because the pandemic claimed so many people. The Augustinians granted approval for the construction of a new home on General Borromeo Street in Barangay Longos to house the orphans left behind by that pandemic.

Supporting details:

In order to cover the project's estimated two-hundred seventy-five thousand peso total cost, the Augustinian council advanced eighty-seven thousand pesos. Along with the land, Doa Crisanta Tongco also gave forty-thousand pesos in cash. Planned by architect Jesus Hervas.

The  Asilo De Huerfanos was established as an orphanage and a school for boys and girls on the year 1890. It contained a library, sewing and embroidery stations for girls, a printing machine, lithography and binding room, large rooms for stores and labs.

The revolutionary journals purportedly printed at the  Asilo De Huerfanos printing press are the subject of conflicting accounts. Fr. The first editions of "La Independencia" were printed at the ASILO, according to Pedro Galende, the author of a book on Augustinian churches in the Philippines.

However, Nonoy Marcelo claimed in his book on Malabon that "La Libertad" was the publication that took place there and that General Luna merely inserted  Asilo De Huerfanos address on the masthead of "La Independencia" in order to deceive the Americans in 1898.

The same thing Marcelo mentioned about "La Independencia" was also stated in a different article by Prof. Ricardo T. Jose, who also noted that the publication was actually based in Manila.

The revolution started to unfold just as the Augustinians were getting the printing press going. The structure was taken by the forces of General Emilio Aguinaldo. The structures were destroyed in a combat between Filipino and American forces later in 1899.

12. Ang karapatang humanap ng asilo


Ang asilo ay isang lugar upang puntahan para sa proteksyon. Halimbawa, ang isang ampon ay puwdeng nagmula sa bahay-ampunan o asilo. Karaniwan ng nagtataga ang isang pamahalaan ng asilo para sa mga may espesyal na mga pangangailangan halimbaw aay para sa mga may kapansanan, sa mga may sakit o sa mga may edad. Yamang nangangailangan sila ng proteksyon mula sa panganib, krimen o panlalamang ng mga tao, sila ay inaalagaan at binibigyan ng normal na buhay kasama ang kapwa may kaparehong kalagayan.

Kung wala ang mga ito sa komunidad, tiyak na mdadama ang epekto nito sa maglaon yamang bababa ang productivity ng tao, tataas ang kahirapan at tataas ang krimen.
