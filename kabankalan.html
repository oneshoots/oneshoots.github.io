11. A car is travelling from kabankalan to bacolod. The distance is 90 kilometers. It took the car 2 hours from the starting place to its destination. What is the distance of the car​


Answer:

The distance of the car is 90 kilometers.

Explanation:

The problem states that a car is traveling from Kabankalan to Bacolod, which are two places located at a certain distance from each other. The distance between these two places is given as 90 kilometers. It is also mentioned that the car took 2 hours to reach its destination from the starting place, which is not explicitly stated in the problem.

To find the distance traveled by the car, we can use the formula:

distance = speed x time

Here, we do not have information about the speed of the car, but we can calculate it by dividing the distance by the time taken:

speed = distance / time

speed = 90 km / 2 hours

speed = 45 km/h

Now, we can use the calculated speed and the given time to find the distance traveled by the car:

distance = speed x time

distance = 45 km/h x 2 hours

distance = 90 km

Therefore, the distance traveled by the car from Kabankalan to Bacolod is 90 kilometers.
