22. JENNY ON EARTH, HAS A WEIGHT OF 700N. WHAT IS JENNYS MASS?


#BrainlyFast

Problem:

JENNY ON EARTH, HAS A WEIGHT OF 700N. WHAT IS JENNYS MASS?

Note:

The gravitational acceleration on Earth is 9.8 m/s². The formula for Force is  ( F = m × a ), and to solve for mass we can have the formula ( m = F / a )

Given Data:

a ( acceleration ) = 9.8 m/s²

F ( Force ) = 700 N or 700 kg·m/s²

m ( mass ) = ? unknown

Solution:

We can use the formula of Force ( F = m × a ), and to solve for mass we can have the formula ( m = F / a )

m = F / a

 = 700 kg·m/s² / 9.8 m/s²

 = 71.43 kg

Answer:

Jenny's mass on Earth will be equal to  71.43 kg
