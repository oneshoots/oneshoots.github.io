Answer:

The Base Protocol on Liability and Redress to the Base Convention refers to the international legal framework that governs the responsibility and compensation for harm caused by environmental damage. It outlines the rules and principles for determining the liability of operators and activities that result in environmental harm, and establishes the procedures for providing adequate and effective redress to those affected by environmental damage. This protocol aims to ensure that the liability for environmental damage is established and that compensation is provided in a timely and transparent manner. It also aims to promote the use of preventive measures to minimize the risk of environmental harm, and to enhance cooperation between countries to address transboundary environmental harm.

Area of Trapezoid = ½(b1 + b2)h

where,

Area = 224 cm²

b1 = base #1 = 20 cm

b2 = base #2 = 12 cm

h = ?

solve for h,

Area of Trapezoid = ½(b1 + b2)h

224 = ½(20 + 12)h

224 = ½(32)h

224 = 16h

h = 224/16

h = 14

So the correct answer is letter D. 14 cm
