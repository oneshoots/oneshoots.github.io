21. Sandy used 1/4 of his money to buy rice. After buying the rice, he then spent all the money left to buy slippers that were on sale at a 50% discount. If the original price of the slippers was 120 pesos. How much money did he have at the start?


Answer:

Sandy had 80 pesos.

Step-by-step explanation:

Amount of money at the start: x

Amount of rice: (1/4)x or x/4

Money left: (1 - 1/4) = (3/4)x or 3x/4

Money left = 50%(120)

Solve for the amount of money Sandy had:

(3/4)x = 50%(120)

3x/4 = 60

3x = (60)(4)

3x/3 = 240/3

x = 80

Sandy had 80 pesos.
