5. 2.) 40°C to °F with sulotion​


Answer:

40 °C = 104 °F

Step-by-step explanation:

The C to F formula is (C × 9/5) + 32 = F. When we enter 40 for C in the formula, we get (40 × 9/5) + 32 = F.

40 x 9 = 360

360 / 5 = 72

72 + 32 = 104

Therefore, the answer to 40 C to F is 104 which can be written as follows:

40 °C = 104 °F
