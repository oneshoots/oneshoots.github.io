9. What will happen to a bowl of sand when mixed with brown sugar? A. There will be no change. B. The sand will not mix with brown sugar. C. Sand and sugar will partially mix. D. Sand and sugar will not be distinguished when mixed.


Answer:

A. There will be no change.

Explanation:

When we mix sand and water, no reaction takes place. The sand simply settles down at the bottom of the water container. This is because sand is heavier than water and hence cannot float in water. The sand can be separated from the water by simple filtration and can be obtained in its original condition after drying.
