3. (G R E S A method)a motorcycle has a mass of 50kg is acted upon with a force of 110N. what will be the acceleration of motor?​​​


#BrainlyFast

Problem:

a motorcycle has a mass of 50kg is acted upon with a force of 110N. what will be the acceleration of motor?​​

​Note:

The formula of Force is ( F = m × a ), and by the derivation of formula we can have the formula for acceleration which is ( a = F / m  ).

Given Data:

m ( mass ) = 50 kg

F ( Force ) = 110 N or 110 kg·m/s²

a ( acceleration ) = ? unknown

Solution:

We can use the formula of Force ( F = m × a ), and to solve for acceleration we can have the formula ( a = F / m )

a = F / m

 = 110 kg·m/s² / 50 kg

 = 2.2 m/s²

Answer:

The acceleration of the motor is equal to 2.2 m/s²

22. problem #1: a 65-kg motorcycle travels with a constant speed of 30m/s what is the kenetic energy of the motorcycle?


Answer:

KE = 29,250 J

Explanation:

Given:

m = 65 kg

v = 30 m/s

Required:

KE = ?

Solution:

KE = ½mv²

KE = ½(65 kg)(30 m/s)²

KE = ½(65 kg)(900 m²/s²)

KE = 29,250 kg•m²/s²

KE = 29,250 J
