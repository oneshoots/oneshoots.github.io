Lac Long Quan represents the importance of aquatic resources while Au Co, the heroine/fairy, symbolizes the significance of the highlands/forests.

Answer:

The linear correlation between the product cost and the value of production equipment is low. This is because the cost of production is only one factor out of many that comes into play when calculating the output value of an enterprise. Other factors such as labor costs, overhead expenses, raw material costs, and marketing costs are all equally important in determining the overall output value of the enterprise. Therefore, while there is likely to be some correlation between the two variables, it will not be strong.
