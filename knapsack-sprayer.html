1. advantages of plastic knapsack sprayer​


Answer:

A knapsack sprayer is reliable, efficient, and durable. It is always dependable and can meet a variety of requirements. You can find battery-operated versions, as well, ensuring that you get a boost of energy whenever you need it. These can save you time because they pressurise automatically. In most cases, a single charge gives hours of usage, ensuring that long jobs can be completed with one battery charge. Along with such, you can also find high-pressure sprayers that sit on the back. Each version available can spray differently based on the nozzles available. You can also find a variety of accessories to make it even more efficient.

20. 4. If price of insecticide is P5,000.00 per liter. How much would it cost?Given: Price of insecticide per liter = P 5,000.00No. of liter per load of knapsack sprayer = .04 LNo. of loads needed = 25 loadsFormula: Total amount of insecticide needed for 25 loads = No. of liter per load xNo. of loads neededSolution: ano sagot:(​


(Given)

Price = 5,000

No. of Liters= 0.04

No. of loads needed= 25

Cost= 5,000

(Solution)

P x L x Q = C

5,000 x 0.04 = 200 x 25 = P 5,000

or

L x P x Q = C

0.04x25 = 1 x 5,000 = P 5,000

Therefore, the cost of the insecticide is 5,000 pesos.

5. Disc plow - used to till ground where the crops will be planted

6. Water hose - made out of plastic where you water your lawn

7. Native harrow - used to pound the soil

8. Trailer - a huge vehicle that moves and carries agricultural crops.

9. Grass cutter - as the name suggest, it is used to cut grass

10. Rice harvester - a machine that helps in harvesting rice

11. Hand cultivator - used to remove weeds by hand

12. Tractor - a vehicle used for easier implementing, cultivating, fertilizing, and planting crops

13. Wheel barrow - a two-wheeled cart that's used to carry and transfer dirt, and rocks

14. Crowbar - commonly used for breaking things, and removing nails
