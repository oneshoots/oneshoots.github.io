Disadvantages:

• These method ignores demenad and the price elasticity of demand.

• Ignores the competitive situation e.g what competitors are charging.

13. 1. The cost price of an antique chair is P3,000.00. What is the selling price and mark-up if the sellerwants a 15% markup based on cost?​


Answer:

The selling price is P3,450 and the mark-up based on cost is P450.

Step-by-step explanation:

To get the value of the mark-up, multiply the cost to 15%.

3,000 x 0.15 = 450

To get the selling price, add the mark-up to the cost price.

3,000 + 450 = 3,450

Therefore, the selling price is P3,450 and the mark-up based on cost is P450.
