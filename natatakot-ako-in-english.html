23. anung English sa natatakot​


English Translation of Natatakot

Ang Ingles na salin ng natatakot ay afraid o scared. Maaari rin itong maisalin sa salitang anxious, worried, feared at frightened.

Sa Pangungusap:

Pinatay namin ang telebisyon dahil natatakot na ang aming bunso sa palabas.

He felt afraid about losing his job.

I can't stop feeling anxious about the results of my grades.

My mother became worried that my brother can't earn a living.
