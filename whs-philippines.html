4. Examples of Formulating WH-Questions​


Answer: WH question is fundamental in every day situations. It paves the way to successful communication along with people.

Here are some examples:

WHAT:

-this is used to ask information about something

Ex. What is the model of phone you are using?

WHO:

- this is used to ask for a name of a person or subject

Ex. Who is our English teacher this school year?

WHERE:

-this is used to ask information about places.

Ex. Where is my book?

WHY:

- This is used to ask for a reason or explanation about something.

Ex. Why do we have to clean our rooms?

WHEN:

- this is used to ask about the time.

Ex. When are you going to manila?
