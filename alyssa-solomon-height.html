Problem: Alyssa is seven years older than Jane. Five years ago, Alyssa was twice as old as Jane was. How old is Alyssa now?

Representation:

1. Alyssa

2. Jane

Present age

1. x

2. y

Past age (5 years ago)

1. x - 5

2. y - 5

Equation:

x = y + 6

x - 5 = 2(y - 5)

x - 5 = 2y - 10

Solution:

(y + 7) - 5 = 2y - 10

y + 2 = 2y - 10

y + 2 - 2y = -10

-y + 2 = - 10

-y = -10 - 2

-y = -12

(-y) • -1 = (-12) • -1

y = (-12) • -1

y = 12

x = y + 7

x = (12) + 7

x = 19

Answer:

Alyssa is now 19 years old.
