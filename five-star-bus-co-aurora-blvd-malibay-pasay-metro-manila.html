2. The distance from Baguio to Metro Manila is 250 kilometers. How long will a bus travelling at 40km/hr to reach Baguio to Metro Manila?​


Distance, Speed, and Time

Formulas:

Distance = Speed × Time

Speed = Distance / Time

Time = Distance / Speed

________________________________

For the problem, we will use Time = Distance / Speed. Substitute the given into the formula.

Time = Distance / SpeedTime = 250 / 40Time = 6.25

Answer

Therefore, it will take 6.25 hours for the bus to reach Metro Manila from Baguio.

Makati, formally the Metropolis of Makati, is a first-class highly urbanized city in the Philippines' National Capital Region.

Makati is the Philippines' financial capital, with the biggest concentration of global and local firms in the country. Makati is home to major banks, corporations, department stores, and foreign embassies. Before the Philippine Stock Exchange transferred its headquarters to Bonifacio Global City in Taguig, the city's Ayala Avenue was home to the stock exchange's largest trading floor. Makati is also recognized for being a significant cultural and entertainment hotspot in Metro Manila.
