30. chemical reaction must be distinguished from physical changes include changes of state such as a ice melting into water and water evaporating to vapor ​


Answer:

The nature of the substance, the particles that make it up, and the number of particles stay intact after a physical change. The properties of the new substances differ from the original, the particles differ, and the number of particles might alter in a chemical change.
