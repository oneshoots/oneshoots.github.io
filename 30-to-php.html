Step 1: In the given case Output Value is 7000.

Step 3: Consider the output value of 7000 = 100%.

Step 4: In the Same way, x = 30%.

Step 5: On dividing the pair of simple equations we got the equation as under

7000 = 100% (1).

x = 30% (2).

(7000%)/(x%) = 100/30

Step 6: Reciprocal of both the sides results in the following equation

x%/7000% = 30/100

Step 7: Simplifying the above obtained equation further will tell what is 30% of 7000

x = 2100%
