7. At a sports complex, the number of children, men, and women who are inside in the ratio 2:3:5. If 25 000 people are inside the sports complex, how many are women? ​


Answer:12,500

Step-by-step explanation:2n+3n+5n=10n

10n=25,000

25,000/10=2,500

2n=2(2,500)=5,000 children

3n=3(2,500)=7,500 men

5n=5(2,500)=12,500 women

Checking:

5,000+7,500+12,500=25,000
