Perimeter = (2 x 8 inches) + (2 x 11 inches)

= 16 inches + 22 inches

= 38 inches

If the perimeter was being measured in a non-standard unit, such as feet, the calculation would be slightly different. For example, if the paper had dimensions of 2 feet by 3 feet, the perimeter would be calculated as follows:

Perimeter = (2 x 2 feet) + (2 x 3 feet)

= 4 feet + 6 feet

= 10 feet

The non-standard perimeter of an intermediate paper can be useful for certain applications, such as when the paper needs to be measured in units that are more convenient or appropriate for a particular situation.
