14. 3. How long will 1 million pesos earn a simple interest of P300,000 at 5% perannum?​


Solving for Time

[tex]\huge\tt\color{orange}{Question}[/tex]

How long will 1 million pesos earn a simple interest of P300,000 at 5% per annum?

[tex]\color{yellow}{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex]\huge\tt\color{blue}{Answer}[/tex]

[tex]\huge\tt\boxed{6\:Years}[/tex]

[tex]\color{yellow}{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex]\huge\tt\color{indigo}{Solution}[/tex]

Given:

Principal = 1,000,000

Interest = 300,000

Interest Rate = 0.05%

Equation:

[tex] T = \frac{I}{PR}[/tex]

[tex] T = \frac{300,000}{(1,000,000 × 0.05)}[/tex]

[tex] T = \frac{300,000}{50,000}[/tex]

[tex]{T = 6\:Years }[/tex]


15. How long will 1 million pesos earn a simple interest of ₱100,000 at 1% per annum?​


Answer:

It will take 10 years.

Step-by-step explanation:

How long will 1 million pesos earn a simple interest of ₱100,000 at 1% per annum?​

Given:

Principal = 1,000,000.00

Interest = 100,00.00

Interest Rate = 0.01

Required:

Time

Equation:

[tex]T=\frac{I}{PR}[/tex]

[tex]T=\frac{100,000}{(1,000,000)(0.01)}[/tex]

[tex]T=\frac{100,000}{10,000}[/tex]

T = 10 years
