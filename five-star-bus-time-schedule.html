Therefore, the new speed is V = 50 + 20 + = 70

[tex]50(x + \frac{42}{60} ) = 70(x - \frac{30}{60} )[/tex]

[tex]5(x + \frac{7}{10} ) = 7(x - \frac{1}{2} )[/tex]

[tex] \frac{7}{2} + \frac{7}{2} = 7x - 5x[/tex]

9. The bus departs the bus terminal every 2 hours. If the first bus is scheduled to depart at 10:35 a.m., when should the second bus depart?​


PROBLEM:

The bus departs the bus terminal every 2 hours. If the first bus is scheduled to depart at 10:35 a.m., when should the second bus depart?

SOLUTION:

• If the bus departs the terminal every two hours and the first scheduled time is at 10:35 a.m, then the second bus depart will be two hours after.

• Thus, from 10:35 a.m, you need to add two hours.

[tex]\large\boxed{ \tt 10:35+2:00=12:35}[/tex]

ANSWER:

• At exactly 12:35 in the afternoon (p.m), the bus will depart for the second time.
