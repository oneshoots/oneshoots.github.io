Answer:

If the entrance fee is ₱50 per person and you and your friend went to the beach, you paid ₱50 x 2 = ₱100 for the entrance fee.

30. Mr. and Mrs. Jain went to theatre with their 2 children. The children were charged with half of the entrance fee. If the total fee charged was ₹36, then the entrance fee for each adult is_


Answer:

₹12

Step-by-step explanation:

since the adult's fee is x2 the children that means if there are 2 adults then there are 2 (x2) and since there is 2 child that means we have 2(x1)

2+2+1+1 = 6

now, we'll need to divide 36 by 6

36/6 = 6

since the adult's fee is x2 than the children then we'll need to x2(6) for 2 times

6 x 2 = 12(Mr.Jain fee)

6 x 2 = 12(Mrs.Jain fee)

6 x 1 = 6(Child number 1 fee)

6 x 1 = 6(Child number 2 fee)

12 + 12 = 24 + 6 = 30 + 6 = 36(Total fee)
