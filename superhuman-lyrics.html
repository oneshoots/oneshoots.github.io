10. In a bizarre but harmless accident superhuman fell from the top of the Eiffel tower. How fast was the superhuman travelling when he hit the ground 7.8 seconds after falling?​


Answer:

76.44 m/s

Explanation:

For a free-falling body with no initial velocity:

v = gt

where:

v = final velocity

g = gravitational acceleration

t = time

Given:

g = 9.8 m/s²

t = 7.8 s

Solution:

v = gt

v = (9.8)(7.8)

v = 76.44 m/s
