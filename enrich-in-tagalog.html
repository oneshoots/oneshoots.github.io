Pure substances are homogeneous in appearance. During boiling, the temperature of a pure substance changes at the start then it becomes constant. During melting, a substance melts completely within a short time.

To determine a liquid sample if it is a pure substance, you should test it using its boiling point; while if it is a solid sample, the melting point is the best way to test.
