20. Tom's monthly allowance is $k. Jerry's monthly allowance is 3 times that of Tom's. Danny's monthly allowance is $20 more than Jerry's. Their total allowance is $104. What is Danny's monthly allowance ? *


Answer:

Danny's Monthly Allowance = $56

#BRAINLYFAST

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Tom's monthly allowance = x

Jerry's monthly allowance = 3 times of Tom's

Danny's monthly allowance = $20 more than Jerry's

Total Allowance = $104

To further simplify the given, we can say that:

Tom's monthly allowance = x

Jerry's monthly allowance = 3x

Danny's monthly allowance = 3x + 20

Since their total allowance is $104, we can say that the sum of their allowances equals to $104.

x + 3x + 3x + 20 = 104

We further simplify the equation into:

7x = 104 - 20

7x = 84

x = 12

Since we now have the value of x, we substitute x in the equation in getting Danny's monthly allowance.

Danny's monthly allowance = 3x + 20

Danny's monthly allowance = 3(12) + 20

Danny's monthly allowance = 36 + 20 = $56

Therefore, Danny's monthly allowance is $56.
