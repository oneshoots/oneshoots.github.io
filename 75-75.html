18. Find each percentage of 75. Explain your reasoning. What is 10% of 75? What is 1% of 75? What is 0.1% of 75? What is 0.5% of 75?


To get the answer we need to convert those percentage into decimal form and multiply it by 75.

In transforming percentage to decimal the percentage sign will be a decimal point which will be going to jump 2 times to the left thus 10% will become .10 or 0.10 or .1 or 0.1, 1% will become .01 or 0.01,0.1% is still need to be converted so .001 or 0.001 and 0.5% will become .005 or 0.005.

[tex]0.10 \times 75 = 7.5[/tex]

[tex]0.1 \times 75 = 0.75[/tex]

[tex]0.01 \times 75 = 0.075[/tex]

[tex]0.05 \times 75 = 0.375[/tex]
