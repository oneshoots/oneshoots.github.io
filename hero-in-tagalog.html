17. differentiate hero from epic hero​


Note that epic heroes do not have godlike power, but they are smarter, braver, and stronger than the average person. The epic hero is usually on a quest or voyage, where he faces trials and adversaries that change him in ways that reflect the morals and values of the society from which he came.

26. differentiate the national hero and ordinary hero


Answer:

national hero -

A hero is a person who is admired and acknowledged for their courage, outstanding achievements, and noble qualities. A National Hero is someone who beyond that has made significant positive contributions to the growth and development of society, and represents all of us.

ordinary hero -

A hero is an ordinary individual who finds the strength to persevere and endure in spite of overwhelming obstacles.
