Industrial : relating to or denoting a type of harsh, uncompromising rock music incorporating sounds resembling those produced by industrial machinery.

Rave : speak or write about someone or something with great enthusiasm or admiration.

Genial : (especially of air or climate) pleasantly mild and warm.
