2. Knowing there will be no punishing consequences, would a good person quickly turn into a scoundrel?


Answer:

Without the threat of punishment, would a good person quickly turn into a scoundrel? I don't believe so. I think that people are innately good and it takes more than the absence of consequences to turn them into scoundrels. It takes a conscious decision to be bad.
