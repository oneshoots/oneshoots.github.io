9. In a condominium with 105 households, only 4/7 of the number of households received cash assistance from the government. How many households did not receive cash assistance?​


Answer:

Find the total number of households that received cash assistance from the government, then subtract it from the total number of households to find, how many households did not receive cash assistance.

105•4/7

105/1•4/7= 420/7

Convert to a mixed number:

420/7= 60 ( the households that received cash assistance from the government)

105-60= 45 ( the households that did not received cash assistance from the government)

Final Answer:

45 households
