(price last week) × (1 - 0.27) = $15 . . . . factor out price

(price last week) = $15/0.73 . . . . . . . . . divide by the coefficient of price

The price of a fan when decreased by 25% was ₱90. What was its original price?

SOLUTION

Let x be the original price.

Then (100% - 25%) of x = 90

75/100 x = 90

x = 90 • 100/75

= 120

Its original price was ₱120.
