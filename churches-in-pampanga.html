Si Lucia ng Syracuse (283–304), na kilala rin bilang Saint Lucy o Saint Lucia (Latin: Sancta Lucia), ay isang martir na Kristiyano na namatay sa panahon ng Pag-uusig sa Diocletianic. Pinarangalan siya bilang isang santo ng Roman Catholic, Anglican, Lutheran, at Orthodox Chapters. Answer:

4. D. Spanish Gate

The Spanish gate served as the West Gate of the ars_enal and faced the Spanish-era settlement of Olongapo. A high wall of locally quarried stone connected it to the south gate that faced the waterfront.

In addition to being used as the main entrance and exit to the naval station, the gate was also used as a jail during both the Spanish and the American occupation.

5. B. Parish of the Three Kings

The National Shrine of La Virgen Divina Pastora, known canonically as the Three Kings Parish, is a shrine in Gapan City in the Philippines that was founded in 1589. It is one of the oldest Roman Catholic churches in the country, and the oldest and the biggest colonial church in Nueva Ecija.

27. ano ang history ng sta. mmonica parish church ng minalin pampanga philippines? tagalog po please​


Answer:

Ang Santa Monica Parish Church (Espanyol: Iglesia Parroquial de Santa Mónica), karaniwang kilala bilang Minalin Church, ay isang simbahan sa Baroque, na matatagpuan sa lugar ng poblacion ng San Nicolas sa Minalin, Pampanga, Pilipinas. Ang simbahan, na itinayo noong panahon ng Espanya, ay idineklarang isang National Cultural Treasure ng National Commission for Culture and the Arts at National Museum of the Philippines noong Agosto 27, 2011, isa sa 37 mga simbahan sa bansa na iginawad ang karangalang iyon.
