1. Which of the following is not true about the nature of sociology? a. Sociology is science.b. Sociology is a social science.c. Sociology is a physical science.d. Sociology is a categorical discipline.


It is wrong to say that sociology is a physical science.

Science is the product of human beings' intelligence as a means to understand the world around them. Science also leads people towards a better future by producing advanced technology, in addition to methods in reaching society's goals. Sociology is one example of social science. Social science itself is the other side of the end of science, with physical science being on the other end of the spectrum.

Among the four statements in the original question, the only incorrect statement is that sociology is a physical science, because it is in fact a social science.
