3. (G R E S A method)a motorcycle has a mass of 50kg is acted upon with a force of 110N. what will be the acceleration of motor?​​​


#BrainlyFast

Problem:

a motorcycle has a mass of 50kg is acted upon with a force of 110N. what will be the acceleration of motor?​​

​Note:

The formula of Force is ( F = m × a ), and by the derivation of formula we can have the formula for acceleration which is ( a = F / m  ).

Given Data:

m ( mass ) = 50 kg

F ( Force ) = 110 N or 110 kg·m/s²

a ( acceleration ) = ? unknown

Solution:

We can use the formula of Force ( F = m × a ), and to solve for acceleration we can have the formula ( a = F / m )

a = F / m

 = 110 kg·m/s² / 50 kg

 = 2.2 m/s²

Answer:

The acceleration of the motor is equal to 2.2 m/s²

10. what possible solutions are presented in the report of anti-cyberbullying Act of 2015​


Answer:

The Anti-Cyberbullying Act of 2015, also known as Republic Act No. 10627, is a law in the Philippines that aims to prevent and address the issue of cyberbullying. The law includes several provisions that outline potential solutions to address the problem of cyberbullying, such as:

Prohibition of cyberbullying - The law explicitly defines cyberbullying and prohibits any act of cyberbullying, including online stalking, defamation, and humiliation.

Penalties for cyberbullying - The law imposes penalties and fines for those found guilty of cyberbullying, depending on the severity of the offense.

Reporting and investigation - The law requires schools, parents, and other concerned individuals to report cases of cyberbullying to the authorities. It also mandates the creation of a Cybercrime Investigation and Coordinating Center (CICC) to oversee the investigation and prosecution of cybercrime cases.

Counseling and support - The law provides for counseling and other forms of support for victims of cyberbullying, including psychological and emotional assistance.

Education and awareness-raising - The law promotes education and awareness-raising campaigns to prevent and address the issue of cyberbullying. It requires schools to integrate cyberbullying prevention and awareness programs into their curricula.

These solutions aim to prevent and address cyberbullying, protect the victims, and promote responsible and safe use of the internet and digital technologies.
