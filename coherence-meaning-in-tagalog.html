3. coherence simply means​


When you hear the word coherent being used, it usually refers to thoughts and/or words that go with each other to form something logical and understandable. So when we say coherence, it means that a thing that is in the state of being consistent and clear.

Let me give you an example.

bird             nest            fly          eat         West

These words do not make much sense if they are arranged this way. However, if we use them to form a sentence and apply the rules of grammar, we can form something that has coherence.

After eating, the bird flew West to reach its nest.

The West is where bird nests are found. They fly away to eat from time-to-time.

Birds fly west to eat and nest.
