6. What is adoptive family? use tagalog kindly expand your idea about this po for short reporting of mine sana ​


Answer:

Ang adoptive family ay isang pamilya na tumatanggap ng isang bata o isang tao bilang isang pamilya member. Sa pamamagitan ng proseso ng adoption, tinatanggap ng adoptive family ang bata o tao bilang isang tunay na anak, kapatid, o apo, at sinusundan ang mga parehong tungkulin at responsabilidad na kinakailangan ng isang pamilya. Sa Tagalog, maaari mong tawagan ang adoptive family na "pamilyang pinapalaganap" o "pamilyang pinapanganak-ulit."

Multiply:[tex]4\sqrt{2} *\sqrt{2} = 4(2) = 8[/tex]  (multiply two square roots with the same number inside cancels the radicand: so √2 x √2 is simply 2)

     (why did we only multiply √2 and √2?)

    (this works exactly the same ways are terms with variables. When you multiply 2x and x, the answer is 2x²; because 2x is considered as ONE TERM, you multiply it as a whole)
