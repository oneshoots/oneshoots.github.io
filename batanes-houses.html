11. 1. What made the houses of the Ivatans of Batanes unique in terms of its architectural form?​


The low houses of Batanes, with their heavy stone walls covered with thick thatched roofs are not found anywhere else in the Philippines. They have been shaped in response to extreme conditions of a region dominated by earthquakes, hot-humid summer months, monsoon rains, and severe typhoons.
