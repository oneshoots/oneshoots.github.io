3. a suling, or seruling is a indonesian bamboo ring flute originating from sundanese music in west java indonesian, it is pooular in many other southeast asian countries including brunie, malaysia, philippines, and Singapore. it is used in gamelan ensembles

4. the dabakan is a single headed Philippine drum primary used as supportive instrument in the kulintang ensemble among the five main kulintang instruments it is the only non-gong element of the maguindanao ensemble

5. the gandingan is a philippine set of four large hanging gong used by the maguindanao as a part of their kulintang ensemble, when integrated into the ensemble it functions as secondary melidic instrument the kulintang
