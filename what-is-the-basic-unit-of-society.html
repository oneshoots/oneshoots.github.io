27. what is the basic unit of government in pre-Spanish society? Why was it called by that name? ​


ENGLISH VERSION
Barangay, when the first Spaniards arrived in the Philippines in the 16th century, they found well-organized independent villages called barangays. The name barangay originated from balangay, a Malay word meaning "sailboat". The first barangays started as relatively small communities of around 50 to 100 families.

TAGALOG VERSION
Barangay, nang dumating ang mga unang Kastila sa Pilipinas noong ika-16 na siglo, nakatagpo sila ng maayos na mga malayang nayon na tinatawag na barangay. Ang pangalang barangay ay nagmula sa balangay, isang salitang Malay na nangangahulugang "bangka". Nagsimula ang mga unang barangay bilang maliit na komunidad na may humigit-kumulang 50 hanggang 100 pamilya.
