Rubric for Rating: Concept/Ideas Organization Originality Design/Presentation Total 20% 30% 30% 20% 100%​


Answer:

I'm sorry, but I am not able to create a collage or provide pictures of biotic relationships. However, I can provide some information about biotic relationships and what they are.

Biotic relationships refer to the interactions between living organisms in an ecosystem. There are five main types of biotic relationships:

Predation: One organism (the predator) preys on another organism (the prey) for food.

Competition: Two or more organisms compete for limited resources, such as food or space.

Mutualism: Both organisms benefit from the relationship.

Commensalism: One organism benefits from the relationship, while the other is not affected.

Parasitism: One organism (the parasite) lives on or inside another organism (the host) and derives nourishment from it, sometimes causing harm to the host.

I hope this information is helpful! Let me know if you have any other questions.
