2. holistic health poster drawing


The holistic health poster drawing is below.

5 DIMENSIONS OF HOLISTIC HEALTH:

Physical dimensionsIntellectual mental dimensionEmotional dimensionSocial dimensionSpiritual dimension

EXPLANATION:

Physical dimensions

The dimension of physical health refers to the physical aspect. This refers to more traditional definitions of health such as the absence of disease and injury. Physical health is the most easily recognized dimension of health because it involves the mechanisms and functions of the body. Physical health is the most important component of overall health.

Intellectual mental dimension

Intellectual mental health refers to the cognitive aspect. Often mental health is related to or includes emotional health. Mental health refers more to brain function, while emotional health refers to a person's mood which is often linked to their hormones.

Emotional dimension

Emotionally healthy includes a person's condition in general and psychologically. Emotional health is health about people's moods or general emotional states. Mood, motivation, enthusiasm, joy and other emotional aspects are images that show a person's emotional health.

Social dimension

Socially, healthy means a person's ability to establish and maintain relationships with others. These key relationships are for example relationships with close friends, social networks, schoolmates, workmates or other social elements.

Spiritual dimension

Spiritually healthy relates to religious spiritual beliefs and practices, personal good deeds, principles of behavior and ways to achieve peace. Spiritually healthy is often associated with obedience to God as contained in religious teachings.
