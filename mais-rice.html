22. In a country experiencing runaway inflation, the cost of a kilo of rice increased fifty centavos each day. On March 1, a kilo of rice costs Php 20. a.) Give the cost of a kilo of rice on March 30 of the same year? b.) Suppose your family bought a kilo of rice each day. How much would your family have spent on rice from March 1 to May 30?


Step-by-step explanation:

a.) Php 34.50

Solution:

an = a1 + (n - 1)d

a1 = 20

n = 30

d = 0.50

a30 = 20 + (30-1)(0.50)

= 20 + 14.50

a30 = 34.50

b.) Php 817.50

Solution:

Sn = n/2[2a + (n - 1)d]

n = 30

a = 20

d = 0.50

S30 = 30/2[2(20) + (30-1)(0.50)]

= 15(40 + 14.5)

= 15(54.5)

S30 = 817.50
