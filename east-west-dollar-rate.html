13. The function g(x)=0.6x will convert French francs to West German marks. The function f(x) =0.4x will convert West German marks to US dollars. Find the value in US dollars of 100 French francs.


Answer:

100 francs in equal to 24 U.S. dollars

Step-by-step explanation:

Since the function g(x) = 0.6x converts French francs to West German marks, and for every 1 French franc, there is 0.6 West German marks. So,

g(x) = 0.6x

g(x) = 0.6 * 100; substitute x with 100 francs

g(x) = 60

So we have 60 West German marks. To find how many U.S. dollars are there,

f(x) = 0.4x ; for every West German mark there are 0.4 U.S. dollars,

f(x) = 0.4 * 60; substitute x for West German marks

f(x) = 24

So there are 24 dollars in 100 French francs

Problem 1:

1.) In 2013, a US dollar exchanges for Php 40.00, in 2019, it exchanges for Php 49.00. 1.) What is the rate of change?

Given:

year 2013, dollar exchange is 40 php

year 2019, dollar exchange is 49 php

Solution:

rate of change = change in dollar exchange / change in years

rate of change = (49 - 40) / (2019 - 2013)

rate of change = 9 / 6

rate of change = 3 / 2 or 1.5 php per year

Answer:

The rate of change is = 3 / 2 or 1.5 php per year

m = (49 - 40) /(2019 - 2013)
