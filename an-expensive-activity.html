Utilities expense is the cost – during a given time period – of using things such as electricity, water, and heating.

Depending on how utilities are used, a company accountant may allocate costs associated with utility use to various departments.

The accrual basis of accounting and cash basis of accounting will account for utilities differently – the accrual basis will record the expense when used, and cash basis will record it when paid.
