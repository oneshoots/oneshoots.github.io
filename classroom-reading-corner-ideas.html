30. The corners of the walls in your classroom form right angles. Suppose that a line was drawn from the corner to one of the chairs, two angles will be formed. If the first angle measures 35° what is the measure of the second angle?need solution and answer​pls pasukan na bukas


Answer:

55°

Step-by-step explanation:

If the question dictates that the corners of a room form a right angle and if a right angle is equal to 90° and a diagonal or line was form in the one of the corners and one of the angle measures 35°

You can come up with this formula;

(Suppose that the missing number is x)

x + 35° = 90°

transpose 35, and you will get

x = 90 - 35

x = 55°
