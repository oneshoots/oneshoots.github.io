[tex]S = \frac{n(n+1)}{2} \\272 = \frac{n(n+1)}{2} \\\\272 X 2 = n(n+1)\\544 = n^{2} + n\\n^{2} + n - 544 = 0[/tex]

Quadratic equations in n and solve the quadratic equation:

[tex]n^{2} + n -544 =0[/tex]

[tex]n = \frac{-1 +- \sqrt{1-4X1X (-544)} }{2}[/tex]
