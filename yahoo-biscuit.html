18. Yahoo in Sunnyville currently has a staff of 6200 employees. Next month, Yahoo will decrease its staff by 8%. How many people will be employed by Yahoo next month?


Subject: Mathematics

solution:  

6200 – number of employees in current month

6200 * 8% = 6200 * 0.08 = 496

6200 – 496 = 5704  

5704 – number of employees  employed by Yahoo  next month.
