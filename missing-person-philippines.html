1. there are eight finalist in the miss philippines contest. how many different outcomes are possible if one person will be selected first runner-Up and another will be miss philippines?​


Answer:

There are 28 possible outcomes.

Step-by-step explanation:

Use the formula for combination which is, [tex]\frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!}[/tex].

n= 8 (number of finalists)

r= 2 (number of choosing objects from the set)

[tex]_{n} C_{r}= \frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!}[/tex]

[tex]_{n} C_{r}= \frac{8!}{2!(8-2)!}\\\\_{n} C_{r}= \frac{8!}{2!(6)!}\\\\_{n} C_{r}= \frac{40, 320}{2(720)}\\\\_{n} C_{r}= \frac{40, 320}{1,440}\\\\\\_{n} C_{r}= {28}\\\\[/tex]
