In order to find the rate of the discount the percentage must be equal to the difference of the regular price and the discounted price.

20. the sale price of a ladies bag is 240.00 if this is 80% of the regular price. How much is the original price?​


Answer:

The regular price of the ladies bag is 300.00

Step-by-step explanation:

Let the value of the unknown be "x".

x = original price

240.00 = discounted price

80 % = rate of discounted price (0.80)

Use formula

(Original Price) (Rate of Discounted Price) = Discounted Price

(x) (0.80) = 240

0.80 x = 240

[Divide both sides of the equation with 0.80 to get the value of x]

[tex]\frac{0.80 x}{0.80}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{240}{0.80}[/tex]

x = [tex]\frac{240}{0.80\\}[/tex]

x = 300.00 (original price)

30. What rate of discount is given if a lady’s bag which is sold at a regular price of P 750.00 is now sold for P 660.00?


We are looking for the rate:
[tex]Rate= \frac{Percentage}{Base} = \frac{750-660}{750}=0.12=12[/tex]%
First find the percent of the P 660

P660 divided by P 750=0.88

Convert to percent:
0.88 x 100=88%

Subtract 88% from total
100%-88%=12%

Therefore the discount is 12%
