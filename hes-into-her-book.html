5. Kate has read 48% of her 350-page favourite book. How many more pages does he have to read?


Question

Kate has read 48% of her 350-page favorite book. How many more pages does he have to read?

Given

R = 48%

B = 350

P = ?

Needed

How many more pages does Kate have to read?

Solution

P = R x B

P = 48% x 350

P = 0.48 x 350

P = 168

Answer

Kate has to read 168 more pages of her favorite book.
