A heterogeneous mixture is a mixture with a non-uniform composition. The composition varies from one region to another with at least two phases that remain separate from each other, with clearly identifiable properties. 20. Aling Puring borrowed P 10, 000 as additional capital for her sari-sari store. If a one-time payment is to be made at the end of one year at a 5 % interest rate, how much will she pay at the end of the year?​


Answer:

Payment = 10,000 * (1 + 0.05)

= 10,000 * 1.05

= 10,500

Step-by-step explanation:

Payment = Principal * (1 + Interest rate)

In this case, the payment will be:

Payment = 10,000 * (1 + 0.05)

= 10,000 * 1.05

= 10,500

Therefore, Aling Puring will have to pay a total of 10,500 at the end of the year.
