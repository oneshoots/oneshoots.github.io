30. Convert 10g of CH4 to molecules of CH4​


Answer:

—To convert 10g of CH4 to molecules of CH4, we can use the formula:

moles of CH4 = mass of CH4 / molar mass of CH4

The molar mass of CH4 is 16.04 g/mol. So, we can substitute this value into the formula:

moles of CH4 = 10g / 16.04 g/mol

By calculating it, we get:

moles of CH4 = 0.623 mol

To convert moles to molecules, we can use Avogadro's number which is 6.022 x 10^23. So we can multiply the number of moles by Avogadro's number

molecules of CH4 = 0.623 mol x 6.022 x 10^23

By calculating it we get:

molecules of CH4 = 3.742 x 10^23

So, 10g of CH4 is equal to 3.742 x 10^23 molecules of CH4.
