2. slogan for science THEME: Integrated Approach in Science and Technology for Sustainable Future


The slogan is needed today, especially in the age of technology.

 

Science, often known as knowledge, is an attempt by humans to comprehend and preserve natural facts and events. Technology is an attempt by humans to apply science for the good of all.

Sustainable development is a method of human-facilitated development that makes the most of the available natural resources. In general, progress in a nation's social, economic, and political institutions is seen as a result of sustainable development.

In addition, sustainable development can also help improve people's welfare and facilitate the fulfillment of their needs without hindering the development of the next generation to do the same.

Therefore, the theme "Integrated Approaches of Science and Technology for a Sustainable Future" has the aim of aligning the development of science and knowledge with the needs of sustainability in life. A simple example is the application of STEM-based learning, namely learning based on science, technology, the environment, and mathematics. This approach has the aim of ensuring that learning in the classroom is in line with the needs outside the classroom, so that students can get used to and adapt to the times.

The key element of slogans that makes them effective is their simplicity. The general rule of thumb is “Less Is More”. The use of the word “green” is an effective shorthand to imply an environmentally-friendly image.

This is the list of rules on how to make a slogan. This is not a rule set in stone, break it if you feel like it would make your slogan more effective.

Simplicity: The word choice should not make people who read it need to grab a dictionary. 9. English slogan 15-17 words Theme: science for the people enabling technologies for sustainable development


Answer:

“Enabling the community through innovation and technology; sustaining development by promoting accessibility”

Science and technology enable the people to accomplish daily tasks in easier and more convenient manners. The only way for a sustainable development to occur is to ensure that the technologies and development are accessible to all the people who are part of the community.

Explanation:

The Department of Science and Technology (DOST) held the National Science and Technology Week with the theme "Science for the People: Enabling Technologies for Sustainable Development" last July 19, 2019 at the World Trade Center, Pasay City. This event’s highlights the programs and services to eight clusters:

Food security, energy, and environmentAging society, health, and medical careBiodiversity, and sustainable use of biological resourcesScience and technology human resource developmentSustainable cities and communitiesResilience and innovationEquity and growth in the countrysideInternational Linkages

How do these areas address the needs of the people in the community?

Why are these areas needed to be considered ?

The accessibility and availability of food, fuel, energy, and the natural resources are needed by the people to survive. Without access for affordable supply of food, the people won’t survive.Health services and medical care should be accessible to the community regardless of the economic status. This should be a priority, especially to the aging society who are less capable of providing monetary support.The environment and natural resources are one of the aspects that are taken for granted. The attention it gets for tourism and marketing should yield the same amount of attention to its needs. This includes the support from the local governments and the proper steps required to preserve its beauty and the biodiversity of the resources.The support for researches and further studies is also not getting the attention it needs. To enable technologies for sustainable development, researches should be on top of the list. This allows studies to be conducted and enough data to be gathered in finding the most accessible and sustainable solutions for the community.The development of the cities and communities create opportunities to address the needs in labor and unemployment. This allows the people to have options and be part of the development by landing on jobs that fit their strengths and abilities.Resilience and innovation is the product and comes as the basic needs of the people are met. This allows the people of the community to look for ways that can address their needs, given the available and accessible options with the help of science and technology.The farmers, fishermen, and other workers on the countryside are the foundations of the economy. They work for the supply of our basic needs. They should also get the support they need in order to address the needs of their own families first, so they can continue to work for trade and industry.International relations and partnership is important for development and innovation. With the help of research and science, these relationships can be of big help to enable the people in the community.
