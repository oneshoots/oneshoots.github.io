3. Powerful film of marilou diaz abaya which bravely exposed the deadly practice of using child divers


Answer:

Muro-ami

Explanation:

Muro-Ami

Muro-Ami is a powerful film of Marilou Diaz-Abaya which bravely exposed the deadly practice of using child divers. In the film's plot, the main character named Fredo recruited more than 100 children from poor families and boarded his ship named "Aurora" to fish for him. Moreover, this film - as the name implies - depicts the harsh and illegal method of fishing called Muro-Ami or Reef-hunting.

Muro-Ami Film and Story Features

Aside from being a powerful film of Marilou Diaz-Abaya which bravely exposed the deadly practice of using child divers, Muro-Ami has a lot of stories to tell.

It is a tale about a man who lost his family in a boating incident and sought revenge by destroying nature.It depicts the harsh environment around the children who are forced into this kind of labor.It tells the debilitating effects of illegal fishing methods - particularly Muro-ami - to the environment particularly the sea.

Muro-Ami Fishing Technique

Also known as Reef-hunting, Muro-ami is a fishing technique that was first introduced by Japanese fishermen. Here are some key characteristics of this illegal method of fishing:

Using of portable fixed netsSecuring these nets to the coral reefsUsing of pounding devices to scare the fishes and force them to into the netsThese devices are pounded into the corals and produce severely damaging effects.In the Philippines, muro-ami used children to dive into the depths of the sea and do the pounding and catching of the fishes.

Ravelo created Darna which is widely regarded as a Filipino cultural icon and the most popular superhero character in the Philippines.
