22. give the characteristics of the different types of salad dressing.1.oil and vinegar dressing-2.Emulsified dressing-3.Cooked salad dressing-​


Vinaigrette - usually a mixture or emulsion of salad oil and vinegar, often flavored with herbs, spices, salt, pepper, sugar and other ingredients.

Emulsified dressings - usually mayonnaise-based, it is more often serves as the base for wide variety of other dressings. Mayonnaise based dressings are generally thick and creamy. Egg yolk is an essential ingredient for emulsified dressings.

Cooked dressings - which resemble creamy or emulsified dressings, are usually thickened by adding egg yolks and gently beating.

29. When can you say that a dress is a well-pressed garment?​


Answer:

A well-pressed garment should show no shine or press marks on the right side of the fabric. Have no wrinkles or crinkled areas. Have seams and darts pressed smoothly on the stitching line, so that the fabric does not fold over the stitching line or look bubbled.
