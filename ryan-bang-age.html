3. 1. Ryan's age is three times the sum of the ages of his two sons. After 5 years his age will be twice the sum of the ages of his two sons. Find the age of Ryan.​


Answer:

45

Step-by-step explanation:

3(x+y)+5 = 2(x+5+y+5)

3x+3y+5 = 2x+10+2y+10

3x+3y+5 = 2x+2y+20

3x-2x+3y-2y = 20–5

x+y = 15

Ngayon ang edad ng ni ryan ay 3(x+y)=3(15)=45 (Paglalagay ng halaga ng x+y)

Kaya ang edad ni ryan ay 45.

Ryan's distance (d1) = 52.5 m
Philip's distance (d2) = 37.5 m
Ryan's speed (v1) = x+30
Philip's speed (v2) = x = unknown

Solution:

t1=t2
[tex] \frac{d1}{v1} = \frac{d2}{v2} [/tex]
[tex] \frac{52.5}{x+30} = \frac{37.5}{x} [/tex]
Cross Multiply
52.5x = 37.5x+1,125
15x = 1,125
x = 75

Substitute the value of x:

Ryan's speed = x+30 mph = 75+30 mph = 105mph
Philip's speed = x mph = 75mph
