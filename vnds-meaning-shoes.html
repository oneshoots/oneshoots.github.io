27. Which measure of central tendency is generally used in determining the size of the most saleable rubber shoes in a famous shoe store?A. Mean B. Median C. Mode D. Variance


The measure of central tendency used to determine the size of the best-selling rubber shoes in stores is mode.

To answer the question above, we will dissect the definition of each answer choice :

A. Mean

The mean is the arithmetic average of a set of given numbers. Therefore, the mean in math is often referred to as simply the "average."

B. Median

Median is thhe middle number; found by ordering all data points and picking out the one in the middle

C. Mode

Mode is the most frequent number hat is, the number that occurs the highest number of times.

D. Varience

The variance is mean squared difference between each data point and the centre of the distribution measured by the mean.

To be able to determine the best-selling shoe size, it is necessary to measure the highest frequency of the shoe size purchased by the customer. Then the measurement of central tendency used is (C) Mode
