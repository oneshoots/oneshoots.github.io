5. 01. Identify whether Yoyoy Villame's novelty song "Magellan" is a Historical Primary Source or Historical Secondary Source. Explain your answer. 02. Is Yoyoy Villame's novelty song "Magellan" a credible source of historical details of Ferdinand Magellan's arrival in the Philippines? Explain your answer. pa help po​


Answer:

1. The novelty song "Magellan" by Yoyoy Villame counts as a historical secondary source. After all, it is simply a recounting of historical events based on reports of other historians. It cannot possibly be a primary source since it is not a direct account by someone who lived through those times.

2. In my opinion, it is not a credible source. After all, some of the details in the song were made up so that they would rhyme with a different line. For example, there is no way to tell for sure that he arrived at noon as the song claims. This is also true for this part: "Oh, mother, mother, I am sick . Call the doctor very quick. Doctor, doctor, shall I die? Tell my mama, do not cry . Tell my mama, do not cry . Tell my mama, do not cry." On top of that, it is a novelty song and does not claim to be a reliable source.

30. Paksa ng si felimon,si felimon


Ang paksa ng kantang “Si Filemon, Si Filemon” ay ang payak na kabuhayan ng mga mangingisda sa Pilipinas. Sinasalamin ng awiting bayan na ito ang kahirapang pinagdadaanan ng isang taong nakadepende sa dagat upang mamuhay. Hindi kontrolado ng mangingisda kung anong klaseng lamang-dagat ang maibibigay ng karagatan sa kanya. Lubos na nakaasa ito sa lagay ng panahon at laki ng tubig. Ipinapahayag din ng damdamin ng kanta ang talagang mahirap kumita ng pera kung hindi binibigay ng pagkakataon.
