18. 1. What is the perimeter of a triangle whose sides are 2x2+ 7 cm. x2-2 cm and 4x2+4 cm?​


Answer:

7x² + 9 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

P = s₁ + s₂ + s₃

Given,

s₁ = 2x² + 7s₂ = x² - 2s₃ = 4x² + 4

Substitute the given value of the sides.

P = (2x² + 7) + (x² -2) + (4x² + 4)

P = 3x² + 5 + 4x² + 4

P = 7x² + 9

∴ The perimeter of the triangle is 7x² + 9 cm
